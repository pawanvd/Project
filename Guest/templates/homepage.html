About Us

At Hotel Paradise we have India's Largest Meandering Pool with direct access to it from our luxurious executive rooms. Just 1.5 hours away from Mumbai & Pune, situated on top of a hill with serene views of the green karjat convenient to travel & easy to reach from all modes. Falling in love with supremacy of nature is easy when everything around you is in the rhythm of it. Right from stepping in, you would witness a unique experience.
